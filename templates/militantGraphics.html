Works

cover of Adbusters magazine n° September-October, Kalle LASN, 2016 (This image of Trump as dictator of a consumer world accompanies a special issue criticizing the rise of a new fascism that is at once authoritarian, ultra-liberal, in the service of the wealthiest and consumerism, a society where, for the Adbusters editorialist, "we bury our heads in the comfort of a virtual reality while mythomaniacs capture the imagination of the masses").

Barbara Kruger, Untitled, 1989 (A violent critique of consumer society, I shop therefore I am is a reworking of 17th-century French philosopher René Descartes' famous formula: Je pense donc je suis (I think therefore I am): Barbara Kruger's work borrows the codes of advertising to subvert them: a limited number of colors (red, white, black and different shades of gray), a simple visual that "catches" the viewer's eye, a slogan positioned at the center of the image and standing out against a red background).
